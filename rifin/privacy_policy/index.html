RiFin Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

RiFin ("we," "us," or "our") is a personal finance application designed to help you manage budgets, recurring payments, and transactions directly on your mobile device. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use the RiFin app.

1. Data Collection

RiFin does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal or financial data outside your device.

All information you enter — including budgets, recurring payments, and transaction records — remains strictly on your device and is never sent to our servers or any third party.

We do not:

  • Collect personal data
  • Use analytics tools
  • Use advertising or tracking technologies
  • Use crash reporting services
  • Create user accounts
  • Access, upload, or store your data on any external server

Your data belongs to you and stays on your device at all times.

2. How Your Data Is Used

All data is used only on your device to support app features, such as:

  • Managing budgets and transactions
  • Creating reminders for recurring payments
  • Generating summaries and insights
  • Updating your financial dashboard

No data leaves your device during these processes.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage technologies (such as encrypted Realm databases).
  • You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app or using the in-app reset feature.
  • We do not maintain any copies of your data.

4. Data Sharing

Since RiFin does not collect or transmit data, we do not share your information with anyone.

We do not share data with:

  • Advertisers
  • Data brokers
  • Analytics providers
  • Third-party services
  • Government or legal authorities

There is no scenario in which RiFin can disclose your data because we do not have access to it.

5. Children's Privacy

RiFin is not intended for children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Since we do not collect data at all, no child information is ever stored or processed by us.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect new features or regulatory requirements. Updates will be posted on our official website. Continued use of the app indicates acceptance of the updated terms.
